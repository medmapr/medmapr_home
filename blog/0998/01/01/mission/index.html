Mission

MedMapr is a low cost, highly accurate, and easily deployable method of monitoring and managing the precise location of key equipment, personnel, and patients in real-time, using standard smart phones and tablets.

MedMapr’s location-based intelligence system enables hospital administrators to hone their focus on asset management in order to mitigate the estimated $1.4 million in lost productivity annually at an average hospital due to ineffective inventory systems.

MedMapr’s easy to use technology is cheaper, faster and more effective than all other asset-tracking technologies in the marketplace.
