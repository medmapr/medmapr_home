Asset Tracking

MedMapr delivers a state-of-the-art Real-Time Location System (RTLS) that optimizes tracking and monitoring of all key hospital assets, at a fraction of the cost of current systems in the marketplace. Utilizing Bluetooth Smart and cutting-edge Beacon technology, MedMapr eliminates medical-care delays by providing real-time situational awareness of the exact location and status of critical care equipment, while also assisting patient flow and asset distribution.

Workflow Optimization
& Analytics

MedMapr’s user interface is simple to use and provides key information instantly. MedMapr enables hospital staff to instantly find equipment, track patterns of use, and leverage analytics to identify vital real-time facts such as which beds are currently available for new patients, and optimize design, placement and storage.

Inventory Management

The MedMapr system provides broad, accurate and instantaneous data regarding the current status of valuable equipment and syncs it with existing hospital inventory management and acquisition systems. This data can be leveraged by management to reduce the occurrence of loss and theft, eliminate costly and redundant purchases of new equipment, and to provide monitoring and alerts that optimize maintenance.
